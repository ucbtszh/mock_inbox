Although you’re likely to be already aware of this event, we wanted to send across a formal invitation to attend the UOL/Wellcome Trust Translational Partnership Award (WTTPA) Launch.

Taking place on Tuesday 21st January, 3pm-5pm (+ networking until 7pm), the event will announce the initiatives surrounding our Therapeutic Innovation Networks (TINs); this will include funding calls, workshops and training for 2022.

There will also be an opening address from Chair of the Wellcome Trust, Baroness Eliza Manningham-Buller (previous Director General of MI5), along with translational case studies from researchers across the university and at various stages of their careers.

View the full agenda.
